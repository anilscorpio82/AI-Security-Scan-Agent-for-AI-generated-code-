# Commercial Dual-Licensing Strategy

## Why Dual License?
The **Omni-Analyzer** is dual-licensed to support both the open-source community and massive Enterprise SOC scaling.

### 1. Open Source (AGPL v3)
The source code provided in this repository is mathematically governed by the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0**. 
- **What this means:** You are 100% free to download, run, and modify this tool inside your corporation for development, auditing, or research.
- **The Catch:** If you wrap this tool into a SaaS application, connect it to a commercial network service, or sell it as a product under a proprietary banner, the AGPL v3 *forces* your corporation to open-source your entire proprietary application.

### 2. Commercial Enterprise License
Because Fortune-500 corporations, banks, and healthcare providers cannot legally open-source their proprietary transaction backends under the AGPL v3 rules, we offer a **Commercial Exemption License**.

By purchasing an Enterprise License, you are granted a private fork of the Omni-Analyzer that is completely stripped of the AGPL v3 copyleft restrictions. 
- You may re-brand it.
- You may lock it natively into your zero-trust CI/CD pipes.
- You do NOT have to open-source your company's proprietary LLM prompt engineering or core applications.
- Included API Support & Vector Optimization.
